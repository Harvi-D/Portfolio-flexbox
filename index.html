About Me

Since I first started building my abilities in web and software development, I have found myself passionate and excited about implementing my talents in a proffesional setting. I am eager to pursue my passion with a like-minded team, and to have the opportunity to continue building and focusing my skills. I hope to be able to collaborate with creative developers to produce applications that are clean, functional and well-maintained. My education has not only given me the information I need to excel in this field, but has also provided me with tools to improve how I perceive and approach challenges. My functional understanding of user interface design, data managment, and application maintainance would make me a valuble asset to your team.

I keep my motivation and creativity sharp by exploring outside, spacifically in the deserts of Southern Utah— it's a great place to hone my astrophotography skills and collect striking mineral specimens. My life-long love of video games and sci-fi was one of the major contributors to my interest in computers and programming. When I'm not behind my desk working, I can usually be found playing retro games or reading books by Isaac Asimov.
